7,000+ languages exist. AI works for maybe 100 of them. I'm working to fix that. 

I build speech and language tech for languages the industry ignores—from Tamazight to Judeo-Spanish, Catalan to Hausa, because every language deserves a voice in the AI age.

PhD in computational linguistics, 10 years of working with messy data, reluctant communities, and zero-resource scenarios. I've built the first neural voice for Catalan, led community-powered data collection campaigns, and worked on projects across 15+ languages from Africa, Europe and Asia, including those in endangered category. 

What I do: turn "impossible" into working prototypes. ASR for impaired speech. MT for endangered languages. Chatbots that engage, not just talk. I work with NGOs, researchers, and language communities who need someone technical but also gets why your grandmother's language matters.

Father. Based in Barcelona. Speak Turkish, English, Spanish, some Catalan. Enjoy camping, biking, drumming, daydreaming and conversations about how to build tech that actually serves people.
